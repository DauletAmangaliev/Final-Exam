Shymkent City, Kazakhstan (Chimkent)

City of South Kazakhstan region Shymkent phone code - +7 7252; postal code - 160000
Shymkent (also spelled Chimkent, Chimkent) is a city located in the south of Kazakhstan, in the valley of the Sairam River at the foot of the Ugam ridge at an altitude of 1,680 feet (512 m). On June 19, 2018, by the decree of the President of Kazakhstan, the South Kazakhstan region was renamed Turkestan, and its administrative center was moved from Shymkent to Turkestan. Shymkent was withdrawn from the South Kazakhstan region and received the status of a city of republican significance (a separate administrative unit equated to the region). The population of Shymkent is about 1,011,000 people (2018).
